The objective of this game is to get the pieces back in their original configuration. Where all the pieces are in ascending order and the last square of the grid is empty.

Target position

The pieces can be slided to the empty space by clicking any of the adjacent pieces. This will move the piece to the empty square creating a new empty square.

Click a stone to move

A new empty square appears

Good luck!

The puzzle can be solved by executing the following steps:

Step 1
Get 1,2 and 4 in the top row.

Align the first 3 pieces

Step 2
Get the 3 piece underneath the 4 piece and make sure that there is an empty square next to it.
Now you can turn the 3 pieces in the corner clockwise to complete the first row.

Complete the first row

Step 3
Get 5,6 and 8 in the 2nd row without moving the top row.

Align the next 3 pieces

Step 4
Get the 7 piece underneath the 8 piece and make sure that there is an empty square next to it.
Now you can turn the 3 pieces clockwise to complete the second row.

Complete the second row

Step 5
Get the 9 and 13 in the bottom row as shown in the picture. Now turn the pieces clockwise to complete the left column.

Complete the left column

Step 6
Get the 10 and 14 in the bottom row as shown in the picture. Now turn the pieces clockwise to complete the second column.

Complete the second column

Step 7
Turn the bottom right 3 pieces until they are in the correct position to complete the puzzle.

Complete the puzzle
